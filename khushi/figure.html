    What is the real story of ISKCON?
    International Society for Krishna Consciousness, 
    also popularly known as the Hare Krishna movement is a
    spiritual society founded by His Divine Grace A.C.
    Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada in July 1966 in New York. 
    ISKCON belongs to the Gaudiya-Vaishnava sampradaya, a monotheistic
    tradition within Vedic culture.
